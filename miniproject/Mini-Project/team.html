Our Team

Our team is comprised of talented minds working together to design spaces that honor tradition and reflect the modern world. With many exciting projects underway, we rely on each other to tackle the day's tasks and get projects moving along. We work hard to create innovative buildings which is why we are excited to introduce you to our wonderful team.

Architects

For Architects like us the designing of a new development is not just its building design, but its blending with the surrounding natural urban or rural landscape which is not just the streets, trees, and plantation but the local socio-cultural fabric. For this a constructive team work of Architect, Engineers, Researchers and local fabric needs to contribute tremendously.

Interior Designer

Abelia Group believes in sustainable urban development with an appreciation for craftsmanship. Drawing inspiration from art, color, travel, and early 20th-century architecture. Well-versed in the relationship between historical and contemporary architecture, we incorporate our knowledge into the modern spaces we designs.

Management
