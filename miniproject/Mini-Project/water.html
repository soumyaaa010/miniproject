Frequently Asked Questions

Who is responsible for supplying water to the society?

The responsibility for supplying water to the society lies with the municipality.

What should I do if I have a water-related emergency in my society?

If you have a water-related emergency in your society, such as a burst pipe or overflowing sewage, you should immediately contact the society maintenance team through the e society management portal.

What should I do if I receive a high water bill?

If you receive a high water bill, you should first check for leaks or other water-wasting habits in your home. If you find no obvious explanation for the high bill, you should contact your water provider or the society maintenance team for clarification. They may be able to assist you in resolving the issue.

How can I report a water quality problem in my society?

If you encounter a water quality problem in your society, such as discoloration or a strange odor, you should contact the society maintenance team or the local water utility immediately. They will be able to test the water and take the necessary steps to resolve the issue and ensure that the water supply is safe to use.
