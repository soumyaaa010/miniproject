Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I experience a power outage in my society?

If you experience a power outage in your society, you should contact your local electricity utility or the society maintenance team as soon as possible to report the problem. They will be able to assess the situation and take the necessary steps to restore power.

Who is responsible for maintaining the electricity supply the society?

The responsibility for maintaining the electricity supply in the society typically lies with the local electricity utility or power distribution company. However, the society maintenance team may also have a role in ensuring that electrical systems and equipment within the society are properly maintained and functioning correctly.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with my electricity bill?

If you encounter a problem with your electricity bill, you should contact your electricity provider or the society maintenance team for clarification. They should be able to help you understand the charges and resolve any issues related to billing or meter reading.

How can I report an electrical hazard in my society?

If you encounter an electrical hazard in your society, you should report it immediately to the society maintenance team through our e society management portal by raising a complaint form
